ONYX MUSSA

One of my favorite mindsets of learning is being open to as many perspectives as possible. I always love being able to ask questions beyond the surface level to see how people truly feel about their daily lives. I love hearing these opinions and views because I already envision rich plans of using film to show people better ways to spread love through awareness and mystic realism. One of my favorite films to watch during my freetime is “Scarface’ which happened to take place and also be shot in Miami. ‘Scarface” directed by Brian De Palma is a great showcase showing a somewhat realistic view of what crime and drugs impacted the city of Miami during the 80’s. Showing how a refugee from Cuba rose to wealth in prominese through aggressive drug trading and murder is reflective to what the scene of Miami was during the time. Being able to accurately reflect the lives of any citizen of America and especially the people of Miami if I have the chance too is a prime goal of mine. I also have an interest in Digital Fabrication and exploring the extent of modern technology.

I have some experience with working with code specifically HTML and Python. Although, I took classes drilling both of those concepts. I constantly confuse vocabulary and functions throughout different programs. All in all, I found appreciation for coding once I saw all of the creative things that were possible. While creating this website I was excited to challenge myself in crafting something I could use on a daily basis. Instead of starting from scratch I decided to use a demo that is an archetype for Fashion blogs. I was attracted to this specific layout of websites because of the use of pictures and accessibility on the website. On most websites, there are toolbars that link you to other parts of your website. I like to have a simple layout that keeps everything on one page for the most part. After finding the code that I wanted to edit I started to go through each section of the website first. It is important to see where the image links are set up and the section titles. Once I was able to correct the main headings and all of the image lines, I was able to create my own template. Since I designed a template that would guide in which sections to edit I started with the top of the site. I targeted specific parts of code that contained bio text and title. By editing each section with the same tactics I was able to finish the website with proficiency.
